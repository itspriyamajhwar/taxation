About NCLT, NCLAT & IBC

NCLT (National Company Law Tribunal) and NCLAT (Appellate Tribunal) are quasi-judicial bodies that adjudicate company law and insolvency matters under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC). We offer end-to-end legal support for companies, creditors, debtors, and professionals involved in these proceedings.

Our Services

IBC Filing & Representation

We assist with filing insolvency petitions for financial & operational creditors, and corporate debtors under IBC before NCLT.

NCLT/NCLAT Appeals

Drafting and filing appeals before NCLAT against NCLT orders, including representation and legal advisory throughout the process.

Corporate Restructuring

We help in mergers, demergers, compromise arrangements, and other restructuring under Companies Act, 2013 via NCLT route.
