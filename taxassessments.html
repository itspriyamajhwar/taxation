What is Tax Assessment & Appeal?

Tax Assessment is a process where the tax department evaluates your income and tax return to verify correctness. If discrepancies are found, you may receive notices or revised demands.

Appeals are legal remedies available if you disagree with an order or assessment passed by the tax department. It allows you to challenge the decision before higher authorities like the CIT (Appeals), ITAT, or courts.

Documents Required for Tax Assessments & Appeals

  • Copy of Tax Notice or Assessment Order
  • PAN Card & Aadhar Card
  • Filed ITR and Acknowledgement
  • Bank Statements
  • Books of Accounts (if applicable)
  • Any Correspondence with Tax Department
  • Supporting Evidences for Claims/Deductions
