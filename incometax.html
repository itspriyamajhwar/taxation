Types of Income Tax Returns (ITR)

Understand the different types of ITR forms and their applicability.

ITR Form Applicable For Who Should File?
ITR-1 (Sahaj) Individuals with income up to ₹50 lakh Salaried individuals, pensioners, rental income, interest income
ITR-2 Individuals & HUFs not having income from business Income from capital gains, multiple house properties, foreign income
ITR-3 Individuals & HUFs with business or profession income Professionals, freelancers, business owners
ITR-4 (Sugam) Presumptive income scheme (up to ₹2 crore) Small business owners, freelancers, professionals under presumptive tax
ITR-5 Partnership firms, LLPs Businesses other than individual & HUF
ITR-6 Companies (except those claiming exemption under Section 11) Private limited companies, public limited companies
ITR-7 Entities required to file under Sections 139(4A) to 139(4D) Trusts, political parties, charitable institutions

Benefits of Filing Income Tax Returns

Filing income tax returns is not just a legal obligation but also offers numerous financial and legal benefits. Here are some key advantages:

1. Easy Loan Approvals: Filing ITR acts as proof of income and helps in getting quick approvals for loans such as home, car, and personal loans.

2. Faster Visa Processing: Many embassies require ITR receipts for visa applications, making the verification process easier.

3. Proof of Income: ITR serves as a legal document that validates your income and financial stability.

4. Claim Tax Refunds: Filing your returns ensures you can claim refunds on any excess tax deducted at source (TDS).

5. Avoid Penalties: Timely tax filing helps prevent penalties imposed by the Income Tax Department for non-compliance.

6. Carry Forward Losses: Taxpayers can carry forward their losses and adjust them against future profits, reducing tax liability.
