What is 12A & 80G Certificate?

12A Registration provides income tax exemption for NGOs or Trusts. Once registered, the income is not taxable.

80G Registration allows donors to claim tax deductions for donations made to your organization, making it more attractive for funding.

Why It’s Important

Tax Exemption for NGOs

With 12A, your NGO’s income becomes exempt from taxation under the Income Tax Act.

Attract More Donors

80G helps encourage donations by offering tax deductions to your donors.

Procedure for Registration

  1. Prepare and verify all documents of your trust or NGO.
  2. File online application through the Income Tax Department portal.
  3. Submit supporting documents including PAN, Trust Deed, activities, etc.
  4. Respond to department queries if raised.
  5. Receive certificate upon successful verification.
