Business Loans & Funding Assistance

Get expert help to secure business funding, whether you're a startup or an established company. We assist in obtaining the right financial solution tailored to your goals.

Why Business Loans & Funding are Important

Working Capital Management

Maintain smooth business operations during off seasons or financial crunches.

Expansion & Growth

Use loans to expand your infrastructure, hire workforce, or enter new markets.

Inventory Purchase

Access capital to stock up on inventory or raw materials, especially for seasonal businesses.

Technology Upgrades

Invest in new tools, equipment, or software to improve productivity and efficiency.

Types of Business Loans We Assist With

Term Loans

Fixed repayment schedule for business expansion, equipment, or infrastructure.

Working Capital Loans

Short-term loan for daily business needs like payroll, rent, and bills.

MSME Loans

Tailored loans for Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises under government schemes.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Business must be operational for at least 1 year (for most lenders).
  • Minimum turnover criteria varies (usually ₹10 lakhs+).
  • Good credit history and CIBIL score preferred.
  • Valid business registration & compliance documents.

Documents Required

  • PAN Card & Aadhaar Card
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Bank Statements (Last 6-12 Months)
  • Income Tax Returns (2-3 years)
  • Profit & Loss Statement
  • Loan Application Form

Our Loan Assistance Process

  1. Free Consultation to Understand Business Needs
  2. Document Collection & Review
  3. Matching with Suitable Financial Institutions
  4. Application Submission & Follow-Up
  5. Loan Disbursement & Support
