ROC Compliance Services

Stay legally compliant with timely ROC filings and avoid penalties

What is ROC Compliance?

ROC (Registrar of Companies) Compliance refers to the mandatory filings and documentation that companies registered under the Companies Act, 2013 must submit to the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). These filings include financials, board meetings, annual returns, and more. Non-compliance can lead to heavy penalties and legal consequences.

Why ROC Compliance is Important?

Avoid Penalties

Prevent fines and director disqualification due to late or missed filings.

ROC Compliance Process

1. Financial Preparation

Prepare balance sheet, P&L, and audit reports.

2. Board Meetings

Conduct AGM and board meetings as per company law.

3. Form Filing

File forms like AOC-4, MGT-7, ADT-1 on MCA portal.

4. Acknowledgment

Receive confirmation and preserve filed forms.

Documents Required

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • Memorandum & Articles of Association (MOA & AOA)
  • Audited Financial Statements
  • Board Resolutions
  • Director Details and DSC
  • Annual General Meeting (AGM) Minutes

Types of ROC Filings

Form AOC-4

For filing financial statements annually

Form MGT-7

For annual return with shareholder details

Form ADT-1

For auditor appointment declaration
