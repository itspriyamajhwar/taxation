Drug License (Retail/Wholesale) Registration

Obtaining a drug license is a mandatory requirement for businesses dealing with pharmaceuticals, either for retail or wholesale purposes. The license ensures regulatory compliance with safety and quality standards set by the Drug Control Department in India.

Procedure for Drug License Registration

Fill Application Form
Document Submission
Inspection by Drug Inspector
Verification by Licensing Authority
License Approval & Issue

Documents Required For Company Registration

  • Identity & Address Proof of Proprietor/Partners/Directors
  • Proof of Premises (Rent Agreement/Ownership Proof)
  • Pharmacist Registration Certificate (in case of retail)
  • Affidavit of Non-Conviction
  • Site Plan and Key Plan of Premises
  • Board Resolution or MOA/AOA (for company)

What is Included In Our Package?

Application Drafting
Professional Consultation
Government Liaisoning
Follow-up & Inspection Coordination
License Delivery

Steps for Getting the Drug License

  1. Choose the right type of license
  2. Arrange all required documents
  3. Submit online or physical application
  4. Site inspection by drug inspector
  5. Final approval and issuance of license

Condition for Obtaining Drug License

  • Premise should meet minimum area requirements
  • Air Conditioning & Storage Facility for drugs
  • Registered Pharmacist (Retail) or competent person (Wholesale)
  • No criminal background of the applicant

Types of License

  • Retail Drug License (RDL): For pharmacies/medical stores
  • Wholesale Drug License (WDL): For wholesale distributors
  • Manufacturing License: For drug manufacturing units
  • Loan License: For using another firm’s premises/facilities
