📄 Documents Required

  • ✔ PAN Card of the Business/Company
  • ✔ Address Proof of Business (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement)
  • ✔ Aadhaar Card and PAN of Directors/Partners
  • ✔ Certificate of Incorporation or Registration
  • ✔ Cancelled Cheque or Bank Statement
  • ✔ Employee details (if applicable)

💡 Why PF & ESI Registration Is Important

Statutory Compliance

It is mandatory for businesses with 10+ employees (ESI) and 20+ employees (PF) to register under these acts.

Employee Welfare

Provides social security, medical benefits, and retirement savings for employees.

Avoid Penalties

Delay or non-registration can lead to heavy fines and legal action by authorities.

Boost Business Image

Registered companies build more trust with employees, vendors, and government bodies.
